Steps We Covered

Scanning Products

Our app is going to scan the product and will provide information about how much the product is contributing in Global Warming

Alternate Products

By using Machine Learning model we will show some Alternate products which have minimum carbon effect then the product you scanned

Dashboard For Co2 Effect

Our App is going to show you a Dashboard which is going to be very much informative in terms of Co2. It will show you how much Co2 you have contributed in Global Warming Effect.

Give Us Garbage

In our app we have provided feature in which you can Provide your Garbage to Us. Our Machine learning model will predict the garbage type and qty.

Get Your Reward

In return of Garbage we are going to provide discount to every user on our Alternate suggested Products.

Compete With Friends

In our APP we also provided community Section in which you can compare with your friends and family about how much you saved earth from Global Warming?
